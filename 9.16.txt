Microeconomics vs Macroeconomics
	Micro: The study of how households and firms make decisions and they interact in markets
		ex. The effects of rent control on housing in NYC
		ex. The impact of foreign competition on the US auto industry
		ex. The effects of compulsory school attendance on worker's earnings
	Macro: The study of economy-wide phenomena, including inflation, unemployment, and economic growth
		ex. The effects of borrowing by the federal government
		ex. The changes over time in unemployment rate
		ex. Policies to promote growth in national living standards
